About Us

Bianca Agosto

I am a rising junior at BHSEC. I am from Brooklyn, New York. Growing up, sports have always been a big hobby of mine. I’ve been playing sports from a young age so I was interested in making a platform for other athletes and “sports lovers”. With this website, people can explore clothing options for their sport, learn about new fitness routines, and be a part of open discussions about different topics in sports.
