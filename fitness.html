Abs

Abs help you stabilize your core. It is important to have strength in these muscles. Ab workouts can also help you lose excess body fat. Following these exercises will help strengthen your abs.

       
 
 

Legs and lower body

Having a strong lower body will help you enhance your running skills. Exercising your legs prevents lower back pain, and reduces your risk of lower back injuries. Additionally, having a strong lower body directly corresponds with having a strong upper body.

               

Arms

Working out your arms benefits strength training. This also boosts your metabolism and tones your muscles. Having toned arms can help you support your body weight when doing exercises such as pull ups and push ups.
